PRANAYAAMA

What is Pranayaama?


Pranayama refers to breathing exercises which clear the physical and emotional obstacles in our body to free the breath and so the flow of prana - life energy.Regular and sustained practice of Pranayama can supercharge your whole body.A cycle of pranayama yoga has three stages:

  • Puraka (Inhalation)
  • Kumbhaka (Retention)
  • Rechaka (Exhalation)

Types of Pranayaama

  • Nadi Shodhana Pranayama
  • Kapalabhati Pranayama
  • Bhramari Pranayama
  • Ujjayi Pranayama
  • Bhastrika Pranayama
