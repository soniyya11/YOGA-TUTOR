WhyYoga?

Yoga is a philosophical system dating back over 5,000 years.
Originating in India, yoga is a system of exercise that aims to
promote physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.The physical
practices of asanas (postures) and pranayama (breath regulation)
prepare the mind for dhyana (meditation).Yoga has many health
benefits, including improving flexibility, strength, balance,
and coordination, reducing stress and anxiety, and improving
overall physical and mental health. Yoga can heal the physical
body and prepare the mind to manage the challenges of the external
world. Everything that you need already exists within you,
we're here to help you embody your higher self.

Start or deepen your practice from home

Starting yoga from home can have several benefits, including:

  1. Convenience: Practicing yoga at home is convenient because
    you can do it whenever it fits your schedule.You don't have
    to worry about traveling to a studio or gym at a specific time.
  2. Privacy: Practicing yoga at home provides privacy, which can
    be especially beneficial for those who are new to yoga and
    feel self-conscious about practicing in front of others.
  3. Personalized practice: When you start yoga from home, you
    have the freedom to tailor your practice to your specific
    needs and goals. You can choose the style of yoga you want
    to practice, the length of your practice, and the specific
    poses and sequences that you want to work on.
  4. Cost-effective: Practicing yoga at home can be more cost-
    effective than going to a studio or gym, as you don't have
    to pay for class fees or transportation costs.
  5. Improved confidence: Practicing yoga at home can help you
    build confidence in your practice, as you have the freedom
    to experiment and try new things without feeling self-
    conscious or judged.
